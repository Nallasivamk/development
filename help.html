How to design your name badge

1. Choose your Size and your Border
2. Click on to blue button "Add your logo" to upload your logo .
3. Add your names and/or titles in the fields and PRESS on the blue button "Confirm".
4. Change the colour, font or layout - at your ease.
5. Click on the orange button "Continue to Cart".

Ensure you have to right quantity of product in the shopping cart, according to the number of names.

PLEASE NOTE: create just one badge and supply us with a list of names (Excel preferred).
Click here to download a template of Microsoft Excel for name list,
Once your spreadsheet is ready for import, you can click on Upload

Please, remember to update the quantity in the shopping cart according to the amount of name.
If you still have any issue, you can send your file to "sales [at] namebadgesinternational.com.au" with your details.

Image format to upload when designing on-line?

  • .png 1MB max (recommended )
  • .gif - 1 MB max
  • .jpg 1 MB max
  • .jpeg 1 MB max

    In addition, You can upload a HIGH QUALITY ARTWORK in your Account in My file section (please remember to mention it as a comment while placing your order).
  • .pdf
  • .ai (recommended)
  • .eps
