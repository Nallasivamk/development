High Quality Lens Cover Name Badge

What is it?

As the leading brand of name badges in Australia, AJ Parkes is proud to introduce our top-of-the-line affordable name badge solution for your business - the New Lens Cover Name Badge. Instead of having a polyurethane dome that is UV cured on a name badge, we now have the option of the high-strength polycarbonate Lens Cover which is made from the same material used in upmarket spectacles.

While being optically clear, our Lens Cover is also highly durable and shock-resistant which makes it a perfect choice for your  high quality name badges. Furthermore, our Lens Cover is UV stable and also protects the name badges from fading regardless of continuous exposure to UV light.  And most importantly, it looks great!

Features

Following are the 5 key benefits of the NEW Lens Cover Name Badge:

  • Optically clear lens made of a high-strength engineering polymer
  • High-durability and shock resistant
  • UV stable and will protect the name badge from fading
  • Comes with our Standard Name Badge range at no extra cost
  • Faster turn-around time – same day dispatch is possible for jobs under 50 badges
