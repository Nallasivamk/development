Reusable Name Badge Inserts

Reusable name badge does not require any special printer or equipment to produce the inserts, however Name Badges International offers a quicker solution when it comes to creating the inserts.

Perforated A4 paper is a pre-cut A4 sheet with the exact measurement and thickness to make it easy to slide in and out of the badge. Our custom-made perforated A4 paper is optimised for reusable name badges, easy to use and works with any desktop or laser printers.

Perforated Inserts will be provided free of charge for your first order of Reusable Name Badges.
